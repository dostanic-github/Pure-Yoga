How Yoga Benefits
Your Brain and Health

Research suggests it might have benefits for your brain as well: Not only can yoga enhance your brain function, but it can also change the structure of your brain in some notable ways.

When you do yoga, your brain cells develop new connections, and changes occur in brain structure as well as function, resulting in improved cognitive skills, such as learning and memory. Yoga strengthens parts of the brain that play a key role in memory, attention, awareness, thought, and language.

History of Pure Yoga

Offering premier yoga in Belgrade for over 20 years.

Our co-founders, Peter and Anna have worked tirelessly to make yoga accessible to everyone, nurturing and empowering.

Anna began practicing yoga after she injured her back playing soccer. Yoga changed her life and helped her heal her back by herself after many medical treatment did not help her.

Peter interest and practice in meditation, Buddhism, and the human potential began around 1990. It wasn’t until Anna invited him to one of her yoga classes in 1996 that he began an asana practice.
